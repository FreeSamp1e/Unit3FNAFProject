Some Time at Freddy's

a Five Nights at Freddy's fangame by Nick Sample




"Five Nights at Freddy's revolves around a fictional restaurant called Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, which features animatronic characters, including Freddy Fazbear, Bonnie the Bunny, Chica the Chicken, and Foxy the Pirate Fox. The games follow various characters, typically security guards, who are tasked with monitoring the restaurant during the night shift.

Throughout the series, it's revealed that the animatronics are haunted by the souls of children who were murdered by a man named William Afton, who used to work at the restaurant. Afton becomes known as the Purple Guy due to the color of the attire he's often depicted wearing.

As the series progresses, players learn more about the backstory of the restaurant, including its connection to a company called Fazbear Entertainment, which has a dark history of accidents, cover-ups, and sinister experiments with the animatronics.

The gameplay mainly consists of surviving five nights (or more) at the restaurant while avoiding being killed by the malfunctioning animatronics, who become increasingly aggressive as the nights progress. Players must monitor security cameras, conserve power, and use various tactics to survive until 6 AM.

Overall, the FNAF series is known for its eerie atmosphere, jump scares, and intricate storytelling, which has captivated fans and inspired numerous theories and discussions within the gaming community."
